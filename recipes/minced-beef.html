Minced Beef

Description

This recipe is inspired by Bulgogi (Korean Barbecue Beef), which is traditionally made with thinly sliced sirloin. This recipe instead uses ground beef to make it a more budget-friendly dish. Using ground beef also saves time, because you donâ€™t need to marinate the meat for hours to allow all the flavors to sink in.

Ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Soy Sauce (3 tbsp)
  • Siracha Sauce (3 tbsp)
  • Vinegar (1 tbsp)
  • Cocounut Sugar (1 tbsp)
  • Seaseme Oil (1 tsp)
  • Seaseme Seeds
  • Spring Onions

Steps

  1. Mix soy sauce, siracha, vinegar, sugar, and seaseme oil
  2. Add garlic, ginger and diced onions to hot oil
  3. Once browned, add minced beef and cook on high
  4. Once beef is cooked, reduce heat and add sauce
  5. After 2-3mins turn off heat and top with seaseme seeds and spring onion
